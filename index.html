What is Prompt Engineering?

Definition

Prompt engineering is the art and science of designing effective questions or commands to get better, more accurate AI outputs across all business functions.

Why It Matters

  • Dramatically boost productivity
  • Improve accuracy of outputs
  • Reduce AI errors
  • Automate repetitive tasks
  • Handle complex analysis faster

Real Impact

  • Tasks that took hours ‚Üí minutes
  • Draft reports automatically
  • Summarize lengthy documents
  • Analyze massive datasets
  • Extract insights instantly

Effective Prompt Structure

The Prompt Formula

CONTEXT + TASK + FORMAT + CONSTRAINTS

Example for Finance

Context: "You are analyzing quarterly revenue data"

Task: "Identify revenue trends and variances"

Format: "Present in a table with percentage changes"

Constraints: "Focus only on Q1-Q3 2024"

Key Prompt Engineering Techniques

1. Zero-Shot Prompting

Ask without examples - rely on AI knowledge

"Summarize key financial risks in this report"

2. Few-Shot Prompting

Provide examples to guide response

"Here are 3 examples... Now analyze this:"

3. Chain-of-Thought

Ask for step-by-step reasoning

"Explain why revenue is up but profit down"

4. Agent Prompting

Create specialized AI experts

"You are a CFO analyzing this data..."

Best Practices for AI Use

Do

  • Be specific and clear in prompts
  • Provide context and constraints
  • Verify all AI outputs
  • Iterate and refine prompts
  • Document your process
  • Maintain data security
  • Combine AI with human judgment

Don't

  • Use vague or ambiguous language
  • Share sensitive data with free tools
  • Accept outputs without validation
  • Skip human review
  • Over-rely on AI for decisions
  • Ignore bias and fairness
  • Forget ethical considerations

Case Study: Financial Variance Analysis

Scenario

Your company's Q3 results show revenue up 12% but operating profit down 8%. The CFO needs an explanation for the board meeting.

Progressive Analysis Prompts

"Analyze this P&L data. What are the top 3 expense categories that increased significantly?"
"Calculate the gross margin change between Q2 and Q3. Show step-by-step calculations."
"Create a waterfall chart showing the bridge from Q2 to Q3 operating profit."
"Write a 3-paragraph executive summary explaining the profit decline for the board, including recommended actions."

Case Study: Healthcare Revenue Optimization

Scenario

A hospital network needs to reduce claim denials by 25% and improve days in accounts receivable.

Analysis Workflow

"Analyze claim denial data by payer, service type, and denial reason. Which combinations have the highest denial rates?"
"Calculate the financial impact of the top 5 denial reasons. Show monthly trends."
"Identify which departments have the longest days in AR. Are there patterns by insurance type?"
"Create an action plan with specific recommendations to reduce denials, prioritized by financial impact."

Case Study: Talent Retention Strategy

Scenario

Employee turnover increased to 18% (from 12%), costing $2M annually. HR needs data-driven retention strategies.

Data-Driven Approach

"Analyze employee data to identify characteristics of employees who left vs. those who stayed. What are the key differences?"
"Which departments have the highest turnover? Calculate the cost impact per department."
"Compare compensation levels of departed employees against market benchmarks and current employees."
"Draft a retention strategy memo with 5 specific recommendations based on this analysis, including estimated cost and impact."
