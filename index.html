Executive Best Practices: Do's and Don'ts

‚úÖ DO

  • Be specific: "Analyze Q3 EBITDA variance by division" vs "analyze financials"
  • Set context: Include industry, company size, strategic priorities
  • Request formats: "Executive summary + table + 3 recommendations"
  • Iterate: Refine prompts based on outputs
  • Verify always: AI assists, humans decide
  • Document prompts: Build your prompt library
  • Share learnings: Build organizational capability

‚ùå DON'T

  • Be vague: "Help with finances" ‚Üí unclear output
  • Assume context: AI doesn't know your business
  • Skip verification: AI can hallucinate facts
  • Share sensitive data: Without proper enterprise tools
  • Over-rely: AI augments, doesn't replace judgment
  • Ignore ethics: Consider bias, fairness, transparency
  • Work in silos: AI adoption is enterprise transformation
